frequently asked questions

Got questions? We’ve got answers.

How do I order samples?

To order samples from us all you have to do is do the following steps: 1. You need to create a USPF product. 2. Go to the USPF app 3. Go to orders 4. Go to Manual Order to open form 5. Select the product, add shipping information and hit create. (If you wish to make a manual order with multiple products you only simply have to simply click on the SELECT PRODUCT button again and choose the product you would like to add to the order.) 6. Email customer service to get your 25% off discount for 1 item per month.

How do I add my payment information to my account?

To add payment information to your account all you have to do is follow these steps: 1. Go to the U.S. Print Factory app > Click account. 2. Once you are in the account page scroll down to payment method 3. Now there will be three different methods to connect to the USFP app 4. If you are using a Credit Card, it will ask you to input the Credit Card Information, and if it is PayPal or Payoneer you will be asked to connect your account via the respective websites.

What shipping methods do you use?

You can check the Orders section of the U.S. Print Factory app at any time to view your tracking numbers. Depending on the product we use either USPS, DHL, UPS Mail Innovations, UPS Ground or Ascendia. The following products are sent without tracking numbers: • Stickers are sent via first class mail without tracking. • Apparel & mugs shipped from the UK do not have tracking.

Which Return Address should I use?

We understand that, whatever the reason may be, our customers may not have or want to use their address as the return address for orders. Luckily, we are setup to receive all orders that may be returned for any number of reasons. (Damaged, wrong address, never picked up, etc.) The address for our warehouse is: 125 Newfield Ave, Suite D, Edison NJ 08837.
